# Define Problem Statements (Customer Problem Statement Template)

This document defines the core problem statements our project addresses, using the customer problem statement format to align our engineering effort with user pain points.

---

## 📋 Problem Statement Analysis

| Problem Statement (PS) | I am (Customer) | I'm trying to | But | Because | Which makes me feel |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PS-1: Policy Analyst** | A policy-maker or development analyst | Estimate human development levels for a country based on health, schooling, and GNI targets | Raw UNDP reports are annual and do not allow real-time forecasting of hypothetical indicator changes | Calculating composite geometric indexes across non-linear inputs requires complex scripting | Constrained and unable to make rapid policy assessments. |
| **PS-2: Academic Researcher** | A social science researcher or student | Understand the correlation and relative importance of health, income, and education towards HDI | Consolidating historical datasets, cleaning wide formats, and running statistical packages is time-consuming | Datasets are scattered and require complex ETL operations for standard analysis | Frustrated by data cleaning bottlenecks before beginning actual analysis. |
| **PS-3: General Public** | An informed global citizen | Explore how my country's development indices compare with global averages and track prediction histories | Existing indicators dashboards are static, default to light themes, and do not persist custom queries | Dashboards lack local browser caching and export utilities for personal tracking | Disconnected from direct personal interaction with development data. |
