# Project Demo Planning (3-Minute Screen Recording Script)

This document provides the timeline and script for recording the 3-minute video demonstration of the HDI Predictor.

---

## ⏱ 3-Minute Demo Timeline

| Timeline | Stage Description | Script & Voiceover Guide | Visual Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0:00 – 0:15** | **Application Open** | "Welcome to the Human Development Index Prediction System. We start at the login screen. Our credentials are pre-filled for this demo. Logging in brings us to our dashboard." | Login transition to Home Page. Show header summary cards and the main input form. |
| **0:15 – 1:00** | **Scenario 1: Very High HDI** | "Let's run our first scenario for a highly developed country. We select Germany from the dropdown, pre-filling its actual indicators. Let's adjust Life Expectancy to 84 years, and click Predict." | Select Germany, slide inputs, click Predict. Show prediction score `0.96` and Very High tier in the results card. |
| **1:00 – 1:45** | **Scenario 2: Medium HDI** | "For our second simulation, we return to the dashboard. Let's clear the values and input a medium tier: Life Expectancy of 70, expected schooling of 12, mean schooling of 8, and GNI of $12,000. Prediction outputs a Medium HDI tier." | Click Reset. Enter values, click Predict. Show result card with yellow progress bar and custom educational recommendations. |
| **1:45 – 2:30** | **Scenario 3: Low HDI** | "Our third scenario models a developing nation: Life Expectancy of 58, expected schooling of 7, mean schooling of 4, and GNI of $2,500. Predict outputs a Low HDI classification." | Reset, enter low indicators, predict. Show result card with red banner and health recommendations. |
| **2:30 – 2:55** | **Compare Results & Export** | "Returning to our dashboard, we scroll down to find our Prediction History table, logging all three queries locally. We can clean items or export this log as a CSV spreadsheet." | Return to dashboard, scroll to History table. Click Export to download `HDI_Prediction_History.csv`. |
| **2:55 – 3:00** | **Closing & Summary** | "This application is fully responsive, supports high-fidelity light/dark modes, and runs on an explainable Linear Regression engine with 99.4% accuracy. Thank you." | Toggle Dark/Light mode once. Show footer. End recording. |
